A Model Residential Community for next generation

An Eco-village is a peripheral, agricultural urban community that is sustainable: ecologically, socially and economically. These are three aspects of life that are extremely interconnected. It makes no sense to talk about ecological sustainability when there are no economic and social sustainability.

We can find some model eco villages which take care more of the economic aspect and others which focus more on the social or/and the ecological aspects; however, it is very difficult to find communities which take into account all these three dimensions of life. Our ultimate goal is to create a set of eco villages adjacent to urban centers, which will absorb part of the population, lower the cost of living, supply local food and minimize the Ecological Footprint.

Why our Project is right choice for you?

  • Maintain the community to secure all natural resources like Water, Soil, Air and sunlight
  • Rain Water Harvest at the community level as well as the independent villa level
  • Efficient soil management, by applying regularly organic composts. No usage of any synthetic fertilizers and sprays.
  • Tree Plantation to purify the air and balance the right temperature within the community
  • Harvest sunlight wherever possible within the community, by way of planting trees, maintain gardens and establishing solar panels at each villa to get the solar energy for the domestic use
  • Ideal place for your kids to learn the value of natural resources, which is much more essential for their future than the materialistic assets
  • An efficient waste management system would be in place to dispose all kind of wastes in an effective manner using Recycle methods to Re-Use

Frequently Asked Questions

What is KANCHI Eco Village?

KANCHI Eco Village is a Model Residential Community for next generation located near Kanchipuram, India. It is designed to provide a harmonious living environment that focuses on ecological, economic, and social sustainability. The village offers eco-friendly housing, organic farming, renewable energy, and a zero-miles agriculture model that minimizes the ecological footprint.

What types of accommodations are available at KANCHI Eco Village?

The village offers a variety of accommodation options, including fully furnished rental houses for senior citizens, cottages for working individuals, and students. These accommodations are designed to be eco-friendly and are surrounded by aesthetically crafted landscapes.

How does KANCHI Eco Village contribute to sustainability?

The village emphasizes using eco-friendly building materials, renewable energy sources like solar power and biogas, and efficient water management systems. It promotes organic farming and supports a circular economy by managing waste effectively and reducing resource consumption.

What amenities are available to residents of KANCHI Eco Village?

Yes, KANCHI Eco Village encourages residents to engage in farming and community activities. The village has educational and demonstrative farms where residents can learn about sustainable agriculture. It also hosts skill training centers, traditional kitchens, and organized events to promote community participation and sharing of talent
